Everyone who plays Clash Royale knows a charging prince can be a pain. To stop a charging prince, put something cheap in front or next to him, like skeletons. Okay, back on topic. The objective is to get more crowns than your opponent. You do so by DESTROYING THEIR TOWERS!!!!  
If you get the Crown Tower (The King)  You instantly win. Before you try to get the Crown tower you should try to take down the Arena Towers. Also, you should not do damage to the Crown Tower when you're attacking the Arena Tower. If you do, it will activate a cannon that will blow off your troops' heads. Just kidding. Actually, the cannon is weaker than the Arena Towers. 10 Crowns = a free Crown Chest (howls of amazement here)
